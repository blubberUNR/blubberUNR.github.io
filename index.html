Project 2:

CS 328 - Project 2: Knock Them Down (Angry Birds-like game)

How to play:

Controls:

Camera Controls:

The camera is centered on the starting position, and follows the player's ball after shooting. To see the rest of the map, you might need to use the manual camera.

Press TAB to enter the manual camera. Press A or D to scroll left and right. Press TAB again to enter the stationary camera.

Player Controls:

Click and drag to release. The farther you drag (up to a limit) the faster the ball is thrown.

Levels and Rules:

Level one gives the player 3 lives (balls). Level two and three gives the player 5 lives due to increased difficulty.

To win, eliminate all the enemy cats strewn across each level.

Enemies are eliminated when collided with enough force, and can be eliminated from both the player and the environment (physical objects colliding due to physics).

Map structure objects like walls and floors cannot be destroyed, though can be used to your advantage (slopes, physics, etc.)

Players lose when they have used up all their lives and have not eliminated all the enemies.

NOTE: At the top right there are two buttons, the left one restarts the level and the right one exits to the menu.

All assets besides the ingame music were created by me.

Project 1:

CS 328 - Project 1 Game (Two-player combat game): Ten Hit Shootout

How to play:

Player 1 (on the left) controls their character using WASD and uses Space to shoot.

Player 2 (on the right) controls their character using Numpad 8456 (just like WASD) and uses Numpad 0 to shoot.

Items:

Pink - Health (Restores 3 health)

Light Blue - Fire Rate (Fire rate improved for 5 seconds)

Blue - Speed (Move and turn faster for 5 seconds)

Once a player has lost all 10/10 of their health, the other player wins. To restart, press Space.
