CS 328 - Project 1 Game (Two-player combat game): Ten Hit Shootout

How to play:

Player 1 (on the left) controls their character using WASD and uses Space to shoot.

Player 2 (on the right) controls their character using Numpad 8456 (just like WASD) and uses Numpad 0 to shoot.

Items:

Pink - Health (Restores 3 health)

Light Blue - Fire Rate (Fire rate improved for 5 seconds)

Blue - Speed (Move and turn faster for 5 seconds)

Once a player has lost all 10/10 of their health, the other player wins. To restart, press Space.
