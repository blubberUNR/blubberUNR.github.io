CS 328 - Project 1 Game (Two-player combat game): Ten Hit Shootout

How to play:

Player 1 controls their character using WASD and uses Space to shoot.

Player 1 controls their character using Numpad 8456 (just like WASD) and uses Numpad 0 to shoot.

Once a player has lost all 10/10 of their health, the other player wins. To restart, press Space.
